# Evaluation Corpus

Chosen corpus: AI4Bharat Samanantar

Reason:
- Parallel multilingual corpus
- Covers English, Hindi, Kannada and Tamil
- Publicly available
- Large-scale and commonly used for Indic NLP research

Sampling:
- Randomly sample 500 aligned sentences per language pair.

Preprocessing:
- UTF-8
- NFC normalization
- Remove empty lines
- Preserve punctuation

Limitations:
- General-domain corpus
- Sampled subset rather than full corpus
- Not representative of every production workload
